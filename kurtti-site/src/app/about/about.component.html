Background

It only took one programming class for me to realize that I loved to code. My curiosity for coding was not always satisfied in the classroom, which lead me to explore different languages on my own through personal projects.

Applying my programming knowledge to practical problems brings me a great sense of accomplishment. Especially when the end results of my work make a positive impact on others.

Experimenting with new techonology improves my understanding of programming concepts, and I continually strive to keep myself up-to-date on the latest principles and techniques of web development.
